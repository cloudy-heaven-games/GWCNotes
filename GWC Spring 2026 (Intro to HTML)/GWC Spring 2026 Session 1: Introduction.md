## Goals for This Season
This season, we will begin covering web development with HTML.  Participants will learn how to create basic webpages, with their choice of page topic and content.  Returning participants are encouraged to create a portfolio of their previous work.

This season, we will be using Playcode, a tool that is new to us: <a href="https://playcode.io/" target="_blank">https://playcode.io/

If you have not done so already, we recommend that you set up an account with your chosen email address (always get your parents’ permission before setting up any account!)

With a free account, you can create up to 3 websites on Playcode, which are publicly sharable.  As always, be cautious about what you share with the public, and always get your parent’s approval.
